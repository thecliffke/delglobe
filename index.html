Geoinformation & Mapping Services

  • ✓Web Mapping Applications: Custom applications using Google and Esri APIs.
  • ✓3D/Drone Mapping: Interactive 3D models (DEMs & DSMs) using Drones, GIS, and SketchUp.
  • ✓Map Production: High-quality digital maps for websites, publications, and promotional use.
  • ✓Enterprise GIS Services: Software installation, configuration, geo-database development, and portal creation.
  • ✓Project Planning & Management: Strategic planning and user needs assessment for GIS implementation.
  • ✓GIS Data Capture: Extracting insights from high-resolution satellite imagery for planning and resource mapping.

Topo, Engineering & Land Surveying

  • ✓Survey Control: Establishment of control points and coordinate system transformation.
  • ✓Field Data Capture: Utilizing modern GNSS, total stations, digital levels, and drones/UAVs.
  • ✓Topographical & Engineering Survey: Consultancy for roads, water networks, buildings, and dam construction.
  • ✓Land Information Systems (LIS): Development using ArcGIS, GeoServer, and web solutions.
  • ✓Land Surveying: Mutation, title, cadastral, and sectional properties surveys (3D surveys).
